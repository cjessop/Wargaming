Menu Controls

up & down arrow keys alter scenario selection, Enter/Return key confirms selection and starts the game.


In Game Controls

Time:
	1 : realtime
	2 : 2x speed
	3 : 5x speed
	4 : 10x speed
	0 : half speed
	Space : pause

Change rocket stat tracker:

left and right arrow keys cycle through the rockets

radar range circles:
# key toggles the drawing of radar range.
